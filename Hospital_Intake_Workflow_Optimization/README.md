# Hospital Intake Workflow Optimization

This project demonstrates **project coordination and process improvement for optimizing a hospital patient intake workflow**, focusing on reducing wait times, improving data accuracy, and enhancing operational efficiency.

The project analyzes the existing patient intake workflow, identifies inefficiencies, and introduces an optimized process supported by workflow diagrams, coordination trackers, and performance dashboards.

---

## Project Overview

**Domain:** Healthcare Operations
**Project Type:** Workflow Optimization / Process Improvement
**Role:** Project Coordinator

The patient intake process is the structured workflow used by healthcare providers to collect patient information, verify insurance, gather medical history, and prepare the care team before a patient consultation. ([Curogram][1])

This project focused on improving intake operations by redesigning the workflow and implementing better coordination practices across administrative staff, nurses, and clinical teams.

---

## Project Objectives

• Reduce patient wait times during registration
• Improve accuracy of patient intake data
• Standardize intake procedures across departments
• Enhance coordination between administrative and clinical staff
• Track operational performance using project dashboards

---

## Current Workflow Challenges

The existing intake process revealed several operational challenges:

* Manual paper-based patient forms
* Duplicate data entry into electronic medical records
* Delays in insurance verification
* Long patient waiting times
* Lack of standardized intake procedures

These challenges created inefficiencies and reduced patient satisfaction.

---

## Solution Approach

The project introduced a structured improvement approach:

1. Map the current patient intake workflow
2. Identify bottlenecks and operational inefficiencies
3. Design an optimized intake workflow
4. Implement standardized procedures and digital forms
5. Monitor intake performance using KPI dashboards

Workflow analysis helps organizations identify inefficiencies and redesign processes to improve efficiency and care delivery. ([Moxo][2])

---

## Optimized Intake Workflow

The improved workflow included the following stages:

1. Appointment scheduling and pre-registration
2. Digital patient intake form submission
3. Insurance verification and eligibility check
4. Medical history review and documentation
5. Patient check-in and nurse triage
6. Physician consultation preparation

This optimized workflow reduces manual work and improves coordination across departments.
