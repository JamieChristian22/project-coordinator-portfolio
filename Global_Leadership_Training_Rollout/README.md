# Global Leadership Training Rollout

This project demonstrates **project coordination for a global leadership training program rollout**, focusing on planning, stakeholder coordination, training logistics, risk monitoring, and performance reporting.

The initiative involved coordinating HR leadership development efforts across multiple departments to deliver a structured leadership training program.

---

## Project Overview

**Domain:** Organizational Development
**Project Type:** Global Training Program Implementation
**Role:** Project Coordinator

The project required coordinating training schedules, tracking participation metrics, managing risks, and supporting executive reporting to ensure the successful rollout of leadership development initiatives.

Primary coordination responsibilities included:

* coordinating cross-department training schedules
* tracking training participation and completion metrics
* managing risks and program issues
* organizing stakeholder meetings and updates
* supporting program reporting for leadership teams

---

## Project Objectives

* Deploy a company-wide leadership training program
* Coordinate training sessions across departments
* Monitor participation and completion rates
* Identify and mitigate training program risks
* Deliver regular progress reports to leadership stakeholders

## Project Workflow

The program rollout followed a structured coordination workflow:

1. Program planning and stakeholder alignment
2. Curriculum development and training scheduling
3. Coordination of facilitators and training logistics
4. Training delivery and participant tracking
5. Program performance monitoring and reporting

Workflow diagrams for this project are included in the **workflow documentation**.
